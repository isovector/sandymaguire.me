He was a latter-day Civil Service type—college-trained, athletic, energetic, reformistic, idealistic, and chummy. Dortmunder hated him.

“We were stealing it for Iko,” Dortmunder said. “He’s black.” “Yeah, but nobody knows that.” “All you have to do is look at him,” Dortmunder said. Kelp shook his head. “I mean, nobody knows about him being behind the heist.”
