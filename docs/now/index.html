January 09, 2020
Confidence: certain

(This is a now page.)

Vietnam. I'm not sure how I ended up here, but I'm hot on the trail of a few intensely-burning and unsurprisingly wildly-short relationships. I'm taking a sabbatical from programming due to serious burnout, and am instead playing music and taking care of myself.

The plan is to slowly meander my way down to Koh Lanta, where I spent six lovely weeks. It's the only place that's felt like "home" in a long time, so it seems like the sort of place I should get back to.

Output-wise, I'm currently working on some 2020 goals, and putting together a retrospective of the last decade. The goals are tentatively to learn how to love vegetables, find a location to settle, fill my passport, go on more adventures, and broaden my employable skills.

Maybe I should add "update my now page more often than every six months" too that list.
